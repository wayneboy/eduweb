About Language Shop

Language shop is a small owner operated language school . At language shop we want you to have success in learing a language , unlike other schools you will not just plod through a text book with a teacher who just speaks the language where you will learn a few words and sentances. At Language Shop our qualified and experienced teachers we will take you through a process that promotes real learing and enjoyment where you will be able to use the language in an increasing number of situations.

Pilar Morales

Principal Teacher

I am a dedicated and compassionate teacher and educational psychologist with more than 20 years' experience working in public, catholic, and private schools in various European and African countries. Before I moved to Melbourne Australia in 2018, I was working as a Chinese teacher in Official Language School for five years in Zaragoza, Spain and since the move I have been working as a French and Spanish teacher in primary and secondary schools in Melbourne and Canberra in the International Baccalaureate programmes.

I am knowledgeable in areas including social, behavioural, emotional and cognitive strategies. I get satisfaction learning new technologies and methodologies to teach values and emotional skills through languages.

I improve students learning experiences and outcomes through the provision of contemporary, personalised approaches to teaching, e-learning activities and innovative experiences in Education

I am fluent in Chinese (C1), Spanish (native), French (native), Italian (C1) and Portuguese (C1).

I have a Masters in Languages, Text, and Contexts from Granada University and three Bachelors in the Educational field: East Asian Studies (Chinese) from BarcelonaAutonomous University and Educational Psychologist and Bachelor of Teaching Primary School (French) from Murcia University

Motto: I manage to make my passion my job, it is contagious.
